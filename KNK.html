KOE NO KATACHI

OOIMA Yoshitoki
Ongoing

CATEGORIES
Drama, SchoolLife,
Shounen

DESCRIPTION
A one shot about a grade school class that
accepts a girl with impaired hearing. Everything
is not as happy it seems.

Although this one-shot won the 80th Weekly
Shounen Magazine Newbie Best Mangaka
Award, the vector of the content made it
difficult for publication on any manga magazine
until it was picked up after months of legal
dispute by the February edition of Bessatsu
Shounen Magazine, where it won first place.

This one-shot was recently publicized again
on the 12th issue of the 2013 Weekly Shounen
Magazine and Serialized on the 36th-37th
double issue of the same year.
